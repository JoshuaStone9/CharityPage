About Me

Hi, I am John Stone. I volunteer with amateur dramatics at the Regent Theatre and have spent my career in the car industry, working as a salesman and in customer focused roles.


I started my journey in Stoke-on-Trent and later moved to Newcastle, which is close by and has become the heart of where I live. Throughout my life I have made many friends and hold family as one of my greatest values.
